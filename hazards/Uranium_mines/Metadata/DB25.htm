Title: DB25: Railroad Commission of Texas.

Process_Step: Data locations were received as an ESRI shape file with some attribute information.  These data were projected from NAD83, UTM Zone 14 to geographic NAD83 and the data fields were stripped and reordered to be appendable to the composite database, uld.shp and uld.mdb.  Although metadata are not currently available for this input source, the locations were reported to have been field-checked, and checked through a review of aerial photography in addition to confirmation with field specialists.

Process_Description: DBF and Access versions of the tables were created and unique ICF_Ids assigned.
